Ques 6.

k-means

Advantages:

  1. Easy to implement.
  2. K means is faster for large variable if k is small.
  3. It makes tighter clusters.

Disadvantages:

  1. Difficult to predict no. of clusters.
  2. Order of data has impact on final result.

GMM

Advantages:

  1. Better with models having hidden parameters.
  2. It is more flexible.

Disadvantage:

  1. It is not as scalable as k means.
  2. It is slower than k means.

I will use k means in this project because it will take less time to compute and is more intutive and simple. And the clusters made will be smaller because maximum variance lies in 1st 3 components.
